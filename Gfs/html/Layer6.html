What Protokoll this Layer use:

ISO/ IEC 8823X.226, ISO/ IEC 9576-1X.236

    Voc.:

  1. receiving = collecting data or signals
  2. ensuring = the act of taking measures or steps to ensure that a system or network operates reliably, securely, and efficiently

The sixth layer of the OSI model is known as the Presentation layer.
This layer is responsible for formatting and presenting data in a way that can be understood
by the receiving device. The Presentation layer takes care of tasks such as data encryption,
compression, and data conversion. It ensures that the data is presented in a format
that is independent of the application, operating system, or network architecture.
The Presentation layer also handles the translation of character sets and code pages,
which is crucial for applications that operate across different countries and regions.
Overall, the Presentation layer plays a critical role in ensuring that the data is transmitted correctly
between devices and that it can be understood by the receiving application.
